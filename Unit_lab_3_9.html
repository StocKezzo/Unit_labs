Pasta alla Carbonara

A delicious italian main course!

  • Difficulty:
    • Easy
  • Preparation Time:
    • 15 min
  • Cooking Time:
    • 10 min
  • Ingredients for:
    • 4 people
  • Cost:
    • Low

The carbonara is an italian main dish oriniginally created in Rome. Nowadays it has spread worldwide and every country has it's own version. Today i will present you the original recipe!

INGREDIENTS:

  • Spaghetti
    • 320g
  • Guanciale(Curated Pork Cheek)
    • 150g
  • Egg Yolk
    • 6
  • Pecorino Romano(Pecorino Cheese)
    • 50g
  • Black Peppercorn
    • To taste

Preparation:

  1. Start by putting a pot of salted water on the burner to cook the pasta.
  2. Remove the pork rind from the guanciale and cut it first into slices and then into strips about 1cm.
  3. Put all the strips into a non-stick pan and cook for about 15min over medium heat trying not to burn the guanciale which will result in a too strong smell.
  4. Put the spaghetti in boiling water and cook for the time indicated by the package.
  5. Put the yolks into a bowl and add most of the Pecorino cheese indicated for the recipe, with black pepper and salt to taste.
  6. Whip by hand forming a cream adding a tablespoon of cooking water to dilute the mixture.
  7. Drain the pasta directly into the pan with the guanciale and stir it.
  8. Remove the heat and pour the mixture of eggs and pecorino into the pan. Mix quickly to combine.
  9. To make it very creamy you can add a little cooking water to your pasta.
  10. Serve immediately with the remaining pecorino cheese and groung black pepper on top.
  11. ENJOY!
